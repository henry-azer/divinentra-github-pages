Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Divinentra is a software engineering company that builds privacy-first products and solutions. This website (divinentra.com) is a static informational site. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how this site works.

We do not collect any personal data on this website. There are no forms, no user accounts, no cookies, no analytics scripts, and no tracking of any kind. You can browse this site without providing any information about yourself.

This website does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, or any analytics or tracking technology. We do not build user profiles, serve targeted ads, or monitor browsing behavior in any way.

This site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

7. Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect no personal data at all, this site is safe for users of all ages.
